Current time, world times, tools and time topics

This is the gateway to time around the world, closer or further away from the Prime Meridian.

Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) is never adjusted backwards or forwards. The UK is on GMT between mid-autumn and the beginning of spring. Currently, it is on daylight saving time (BST), which is GMT+1.
